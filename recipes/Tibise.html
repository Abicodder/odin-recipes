Tibise

Description

Tibise is a traditional Ethiopian dish loved by many. It is usually made at holidays and special occations.

Ingredients

  • sliced meat
  • onion
  • garlic
  • oil
  • butter
  • salt
  • pepper

Steps

  1. Slice the onions and cook them in the pan until they turn brown.
  2. Add oil and sliced garlic to the pan and cook it for a few minutes,
  3. cut the meat into small piceces and add it to the pan.
  4. cook the meat for 5 minutes and add pepper
  5. add butter and salt and you are finished
