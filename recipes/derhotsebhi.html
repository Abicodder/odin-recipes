Derho Tsebhi

Description

This spicy chicken stew from Ethiopia isn’t just ridiculously tasty, but also Paleo – meaning it’s gluten and grain-free.

Ingredients

  • chicken drumsticks
  • 2 red onions
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 1 table spoon berbere
  • 4 hard boilded eggs
  • ¼ teaspoon ground cardamom
  • 2 cups chicken stock

Steps

  1. Thoroughly season the drumsticks with salt, and set aside.
  2. Add the sliced onions, and let them cook undisturbed for 1 to 2 minutes. Season liberally with salt, and gently turn over the pile of onions every 3 to 4 minutes to ensure even cooking
  3. Add the sliced onions, and let them cook undisturbed for 1 to 2 minutes. Season liberally with salt, and gently turn over the pile of onions every 3 to 4 minutes to ensure even cooking
  4. Add the berbere, cardamom, and black pepper and cook until fragrant.
